About Me

Hi, I’m Emma - Welcome!

I’m the founder of Becoming You and a Wellbeing Coach passionate about helping women reconnect with themselves. Through workshops and courses (online and in-person) and my facebook group, I support women to rediscover what brings them joy, making space for self-care, and living more authentically. My own journey began over a decade ago when I realised I’d lost touch with who I was. Like many women, I’d poured everything into my family and left nothing for myself. Supporting my son to explore his passions made me realise I had no idea what mine were anymore. Since then, I’ve been on a path of rediscovery, reconnecting with my childhood passions, discovering new ones, and learning to find joy in the little things. I believe that when we honour who we truly are, everyone around us benefits. You deserve to feel like you again and I’d love to help you get there.
